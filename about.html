Material

Charmeuse is a fabric that is soft in contact with the skin, its surface is lustrous, luxurious, and has a graceful luster without the fear of objectionable heaviness.

The luster of Charmeuse is considered to be the ultimate, capturing beautiful light like a flowing water smoothly under sunlight.

This is a luxury fabric that was loved for decades, in the 14th century it was popular across Europe for the aristocracy, the church and the upper class.
